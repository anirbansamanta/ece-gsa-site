About

Electrical and Computer Engineering Graduate Student Association (ECE-GSA) is a student organization serving the department of ECE graduate group. Our goal is to cultivate a community for graduate students in Electrical and Computer Engineering at Davis. Throughout the year, we organize fun opportunities for students, faculty and staff to interact through events such as the weekly coffee socials, BBQs, trips, and more. We also welcome new students in the Fall, contribute to recruiting in the spring, help PhD students prepare for the prelims exam in the winter.
